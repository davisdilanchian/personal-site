How Pareto works

  • Scan a receipt — point your camera at the receipt and Pareto reads the line items automatically.
  • Assign items — tap to assign each item to the people in your group.
  • Split tax & tip — Pareto divides tax and tip proportionally.
  • Request payment — send each person a Venmo request for their share.

FAQ

The scanner misread my receipt

You can tap any item to edit its name or price before splitting. Good lighting and a flat, fully-visible receipt give the best scan results.

Do I need a Venmo account?

Venmo is used to send payment requests. If a person doesn't use Venmo, you can still see their exact share and settle up however you like.

What does Pro include?

Pro removes ads and unlocks the full feature set. Purchases are handled by Apple and restore across your devices.

Is my data private?

Your bills are stored on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details on receipt scanning and payments.
