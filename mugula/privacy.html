PRIVACY POLICY
Mugula — iMessage Photo Editor

Privacy Policy

Effective: 3 June 2026

Mugula is built to be private by design. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, and it has no account system, servers, or third-party analytics.

Photos & Camera

Mugula uses your camera and photo library only so you can capture or choose a photo to edit. All editing — drawing, stickers, captions, and face filters — happens entirely on your device. Face detection uses Apple's on-device Vision framework; no images leave your device for processing.

Sending

An edited photo is only shared when you choose to send it in your iMessage conversation. At that point it is delivered through Apple's Messages, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Mugula itself never uploads your photos anywhere.

Data We Collect

None. Mugula stores no personal information and sends nothing to us or any third party.

Children

Mugula does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear on this page with a new effective date.
