Getting started

  • Open a conversation in Messages, tap the apps icon next to the text field, and choose Mugula.
  • Take a photo or pick one from your library.
  • Add drawings, stickers, captions, or face filters.
  • Tap send to drop the edited photo straight into your chat.

FAQ

I don't see Mugula in iMessage

In a conversation, tap the gray apps icon beside the message field, then the "•••" / app drawer, and enable Mugula. You may need to scroll the app strip.

Face filters aren't appearing

Face filters need a clearly visible face and camera access. Check that Messages/Mugula has camera permission in Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera.

Is my photo private?

Yes — all editing happens on your device and nothing is uploaded. See the Privacy Policy.
